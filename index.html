Platform

Everything you need to
analyze the banking system

From high-level system evolution to account-level drill-downs across any bank, any period.

Bank Monitor
Track key indicators over time — assets, loans, deposits, equity, and net income — for up to 5 banks simultaneously.
Balance Sheet & P&L
Full balance sheet and income statement for any bank and period, in CLP or USD, with one-click Excel export.
Account Explorer
Drill down through 3 levels of the CMF chart of accounts and compare any account across all banks.
System Ranking
See the full banking system ranked by equity or ROE, with period-over-period comparison at a glance.
Credit Quality (NPL)
Monitor non-performing loan ratios and portfolio quality evolution across the system and by institution.
CLP / USD Toggle
Switch all figures between Chilean Pesos and US Dollars using real-time exchange rates from the central bank.
